Hyderabad Itinerary

Day 1: Historical Wonders and Local Markets

Morning

Start your day at Charminar, an iconic monument in Hyderabad. Explore the area and climb to the top for a panoramic view.

Visit Laad Bazaar, known for its bangles, traditional clothing, and local crafts.

Lunch

Head to Paradise Restaurant for a taste of Hyderabadi biryani and other local specialties.

Afternoon

Explore the Qutub Shahi Tombs complex, known for its architectural splendor and serene ambiance.

Spend the afternoon at Golconda Fort. Explore its various sections, including the Fateh Darwaza and the beautiful views from the top.

Evening

Consider attending the captivating light and sound show at Golconda Fort to learn about its history in an engaging way.

Day 2: Modern Attractions and Cultural Exploration

Morning

Begin your day at Birla Mandir, a magnificent marble temple with stunning views of the city.

Explore the Salar Jung Museum's vast collection of art and artifacts from around the world.

Lunch

Have lunch at Eat Street, a popular food court near Hussain Sagar Lake.

Afternoon

Spend your afternoon at Nehru Zoological Park, home to a diverse range of animals and birds.

Enjoy a leisurely stroll in Lumbini Park and take a boat ride on Hussain Sagar Lake.

Evening

Try local Hyderabadi cuisine for dinner. Some must-try dishes include Haleem, Dum Biryani, and Mirchi ka Salan.

Day 3: Palaces and Cultural Experiences

Morning

Begin your day at Chowmahalla Palace, an opulent palace complex showcasing the Nizam's lifestyle.

Visit the historic Mecca Masjid and explore the nearby bazaar.

Lunch

Enjoy a traditional Hyderabadi meal at Shadaab Restaurant, known for its delectable biryani and kebabs.
