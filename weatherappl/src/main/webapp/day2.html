Hyderabad Itinerary

Day 1: Exploring Heritage and History

Morning

Start your day with a visit to Charminar, an iconic monument in Hyderabad. Climb up to the top for a panoramic view of the city.

Located near Charminar, Laad Bazaar is known for its colorful bangles, traditional clothing, and local handicrafts. It's a great place to shop for souvenirs.

Lunch

Head to Paradise Restaurant, a well-known restaurant in Hyderabad, for a traditional Hyderabadi biryani or other local specialties.

Afternoon

Explore the Qutub Shahi Tombs complex, which houses the tombs of the seven Qutub Shahi rulers. The architecture and the serene atmosphere make it a fascinating place.

Visit Golconda Fort in the afternoon. Explore the fort complex, marvel at the acoustics at the Fateh Darwaza, and enjoy the beautiful views from the top.

Evening

If you have time, consider attending the light and sound show at Golconda Fort to learn about its history in an engaging way.

Day 2: Modern Attractions and Culinary Delights

Morning

Begin your day at Birla Mandir, a stunning marble temple dedicated to Lord Venkateswara. The panoramic view from the hilltop is breathtaking.

Explore the Salar Jung Museum, which houses an extensive collection of art and artifacts from around the world. Don't miss the famous Veiled Rebecca sculpture.

Lunch

Enjoy a leisurely lunch at Eat Street, a popular food court located beside the picturesque Hussain Sagar Lake.

Afternoon

Spend your afternoon at Nehru Zoological Park, which houses a wide variety of animals, birds, and reptiles. It's a great place for a family outing.

Take a stroll in Lumbini Park and enjoy a boat ride on Hussain Sagar Lake to get a different perspective of the city.

Evening

Try local Hyderabadi cuisine for dinner. Some popular dishes to consider are Haleem, Dum Biryani, and Mirchi ka Salan.

Optional Nightlife

Explore the nightlife in Banjara Hills and Jubilee Hills, which have a range of bars, clubs, and lounges.
