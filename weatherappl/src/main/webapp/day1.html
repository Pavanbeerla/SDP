Morning

Start your day with a visit to the Charminar, the most iconic landmark in Hyderabad. This 4-pillared tower was built in 1591 and offers stunning views of the city.

Next, head to the Mecca Masjid, one of the largest mosques in India. This mosque is known for its beautiful architecture and its ability to accommodate up to 10,000 worshippers.

After visiting the Mecca Masjid, take a walk through the Laad Bazaar, a market selling colorful bangles and other jewelry. This is a great place to shop for souvenirs and to experience the local culture.

Afternoon

For lunch, head to one of the many restaurants in the Old City that serve Hyderabadi biryani, a world-famous dish.

After lunch, visit the Salar Jung Museum, one of the largest museums in India. This museum houses a vast collection of artifacts from around the world, including paintings, sculptures, and textiles.

If you have time, you can also visit the Chowmahalla Palace, the former residence of the Nizams of Hyderabad. This palace complex is known for its beautiful architecture and its lush gardens.

Evening

For dinner, head to one of the many restaurants in Hyderabad that serve authentic Hyderabadi cuisine.

After dinner, take a walk around Hussain Sagar Lake, a man-made lake in the heart of the city. This is a popular spot for locals and tourists alike to enjoy the evening breeze and the views of the city skyline.
